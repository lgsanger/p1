Hi, my name is Lila Gsanger!

I watched the movie 'Coraline' for the first time at nine years old, and it was love at first sight. I have now probably seen that movie over a hundered times and it will never get old. I have thrown myself into the world of Coraline through my writing, art, and any mental free space available. I hope you enjoyed a little glimpse into my longest and deepest love for this beautiful stop-motion film.
